# StartupAI Homepage — Design Prompts (Simplified)

**For:** Designers, Figma AI, or implementation teams  
**Style:** Premium SaaS, calm and intelligent, editorial quality

---

## Overview

This homepage tells a clear story in three acts:

- **Introduce** — Present the core value and guide users into understanding
- **Build confidence** — Show features, workflow, and system clarity through visuals
- **Convert** — Prove outcomes and lead to one focused action

---

## Style Guide (Apply to All Sections)

**Visual Identity:**
- Light off-white or soft neutral backgrounds
- Deep charcoal text
- One muted accent color (emerald or sage green)
- Serif headlines, sans-serif body text
- Generous whitespace and breathing room

**Interactive Style:**
- Light borders on cards
- Subtle shadows only
- No neon, no heavy gradients, no dramatic effects
- Hover states: border color shift + gentle lift

**Animation Approach:**
- Scroll-triggered reveals (fade + small upward motion)
- Short duration (200-400ms)
- No loops, no bounce, no spring effects
- Calm and measured

## Section 1 — Hero

**Background:** Light (off-white or soft neutral)

**Purpose:**  
Communicate the core value proposition instantly with calm confidence.

**Layout:**  
Two-column layout. Left side: text content (eyebrow, large serif headline, supporting paragraph, two CTA buttons). Right side: floating product interface mockup or abstract visual. Text vertically centered, generous top padding.

**Content:**  
- Eyebrow: "AI OPERATING SYSTEM FOR FOUNDERS"
- Headline: "From strategy to daily execution, in one guided flow."
- Subhead: "StartupAI is the central intelligence layer that transforms scattered tools and information into a unified, investor-ready operating system."
- Primary CTA: "Start Your Profile" (solid button)
- Secondary CTA: "Watch How It Works" (outlined button)

**Visual Cues:**  
Product UI preview has subtle shadow, slightly tilted for depth. CTAs side by side with comfortable gap.

**Motion:**  
Eyebrow fades in → headline fades in with upward slide → subhead appears → CTAs fade in → product visual slides from right.

**Responsive:**  
Desktop: two columns. Mobile: stack vertically (text first, then visual).
