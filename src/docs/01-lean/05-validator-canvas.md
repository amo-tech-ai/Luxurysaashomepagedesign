### What This Screen Answers

### Core Value Proposition
The Validation Board is the **"test view"** of your startup. Where the Lean Canvas says what you **believe**, the Validation Board shows what you're **testing** and what you **learned**.

**Key Philosophy:** You don't build until assumptions for that stage are validated. If the riskiest fails, you pivot (audience, pain, or solution) and repeat.
