# AI Efficiency & Marketing Impact Planner

## Feature Proposal: AI Efficiency & Marketing Impact Planner

### One-line description

A feature that shows founders where AI will save the most time and money in their business right now, and tells them exactly what to build or automate first, backed by industry evidence.

---

## Why this feature (from the report)

**State of Fashion 2026** shows that:

- AI has shifted from a "nice to have" to a **business necessity**
- Marketing and communications see the **largest productivity gains** from gen-AI
- Brands are under pressure to **do more with fewer resources**
- **Efficiency beats growth hacks** in uncertain markets

This makes **"Where should I apply AI first?"** a critical startup question.
