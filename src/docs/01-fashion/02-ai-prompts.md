## ‚ú® VISUAL HIERARCHY RULES FOR ALL PROMPTS

**Always include in your prompts:**

1. ‚úÖ **Oversized serif headline** ‚Äî Creates authority
2. ‚úÖ **Clean sans-serif body** ‚Äî Ensures readability  
3. ‚úÖ **1 dominant visual element per section** ‚Äî Prevents overwhelm
4. ‚úÖ **Soft shadows, not heavy gradients** ‚Äî Maintains elegance
5. ‚úÖ **Minimal iconography** ‚Äî Reduces visual noise
6. ‚úÖ **Consistent 8pt spacing rhythm** ‚Äî Creates harmony
7. ‚úÖ **Balanced white space (60/40 ratio)** ‚Äî Luxury feel
