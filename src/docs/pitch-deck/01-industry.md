# Industry-Specific Pitch Deck Wizard

**Status:** ✅ Complete  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Last Updated:** January 27, 2026

---

## Overview

A 5-step wizard that adapts questions, examples, and AI suggestions based on the user's selected industry. The wizard uses a consistent three-column layout across all steps.

---

## Layout Structure

### Three-Column Grid

**Left Column (240px)**
- Step navigation
- Progress indicator (percentage bar)
- Step list with completion status
- Back to Dashboard link

**Center Column (Flexible)**
- Step title and description
- Form inputs and questions
- Navigation buttons (Back/Continue)
- Contextual help text

**Right Column (320px)**
- AI Insights panel
- Suggestion buttons
- Validation checklist
- Pro tips and guidance

---

## 5-Step Flow

### Step 1: Startup Info
**Purpose:** Capture basic startup information  
**Fields:**
- Startup name
- One-liner (120 char max)
- Founding stage (Pre-Seed, Seed, Series A)

**Progress:** 20%

---

### Step 2: Industry Focus
**Purpose:** Select industry and answer industry-specific questions  
**Conditional Logic:** Questions adapt based on selected industry

**Part A: Industry Selection**
- 13 industry cards with icons
- Single selection
- Visual feedback on selection

**Part B: Industry-Specific Questions**
- 4 tailored questions per industry
- Industry-specific placeholders
- Examples relevant to the selected industry
- Metrics fields unique to each industry

**Progress:** 40%

---

### Step 3: Market & Traction
**Purpose:** Define market size and traction metrics  
**Fields:**
- TAM (Total Addressable Market)
- SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market)
- SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)
- Revenue (if applicable)
- Growth metrics

**Progress:** 60%

---

### Step 4: Deck Style
**Purpose:** Choose visual style and tone  
**Options:**
- Minimal (clean, modern)
- Bold (vibrant, impactful)
- Classic (traditional, professional)

**Progress:** 80%

---

### Step 5: Review & Generate
**Purpose:** Review inputs and generate deck  
**Features:**
- Summary of all inputs
- Editable fields
- AI confidence score
- Generate Deck CTA

**Progress:** 100%

## Responsive Behavior

### Desktop (1024px+)
- Three-column layout visible
- Left nav: 240px fixed
- Right panel: 320px fixed
- Center: Flexible width

### Tablet (768px - 1023px)
- Left nav collapses to icon-only
- Right panel moves below content
- Single column layout

### Mobile (< 768px)
- Stack all columns vertically
- Left nav becomes top progress bar
- Right panel at bottom
- Full-width inputs

---

## States

### Input States
- **Default:** Gray border, white background
- **Focus:** Green border, ring effect
- **Filled:** Gray border, value visible
- **Error:** Red border, error message
- **Disabled:** Gray background, cursor not-allowed

### Button States
- **Default:** Green background, white text
- **Hover:** Darker green, shadow
- **Active:** Even darker green
- **Disabled:** Gray background, no hover

### Industry Card States
- **Default:** Gray border, white background
- **Hover:** Gray-300 border, shadow-sm
- **Selected:** Green border, green background tint, checkmark
